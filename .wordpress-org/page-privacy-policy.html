Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fricking Website ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Fricking Local Business Insights WordPress plugin ("the Plugin") and visit fricking.website.

1. What Information We Collect

Through the Plugin (on your WordPress site)

When a visitor uses the scanner embedded on your site, the Plugin may collect:

  • The business name or search query entered
  • The visitor's IP address (used for rate limiting only; never stored long-term)
  • Lead information voluntarily submitted via the lead capture form: name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields you configure

This data is stored in your WordPress database on your own hosting. We (the plugin developer) do not receive or have access to the leads your visitors submit.

Through Freemius (license management)

If you activate a premium license, our payment and licensing platform Freemius collects your name, email address, and payment information to process your subscription. Freemius's privacy policy is available at freemius.com/privacy.

Through fricking.website

When you visit our website, standard server logs may record your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. We use this only for security and performance purposes.

2. Third-Party Services the Plugin Calls

The Plugin makes outbound requests to the following external services when processing a scan:

  • Google Places API — to retrieve business information, reviews, and photos. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google PageSpeed Insights API — to retrieve website performance data for the scanned business's website
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate AI-scored analysis and recommendations. Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy

These requests are made server-side using API keys you provide. Your API keys are stored in your WordPress database and never transmitted to us.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide scan results and generate AI reports
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse
  • To deliver leads to your admin dashboard
  • To send lead notification emails to the address you configure
  • To process license payments and provide customer support (via Freemius)

4. Data Retention

Scan results and leads are stored in your WordPress database for as long as you keep the Plugin installed. You may export and delete all data at any time via the Plugin's Export Data page. Uninstalling the Plugin removes all associated data from your database.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

6. Cookies

The Plugin does not set any cookies on your visitors' browsers. Shared report links use URL tokens, not cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

The Plugin and our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
